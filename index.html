How it works

Choose your goal

Browse the groups (teams) available and choose the one that matches your goal. Each team has it's own private forum.

Meet your coach

Each team has a coach. Your coach contacts you to create a program tailored to your goals & lifestyle. Coaches manage the team’s forum and ensure everyone progresses together.

Get your program

Your customised program is split into two parts – Fitness & Nutrition. Easy to follow videos and step by step guides provide a fun and uncomplicated journey to a fitter, healthier, better you.

Track your progress

The only way to know how well you are doing is to measure your progress. You’ll do this by tracking key metrics as instructed by your coach.

Why join MyTeam?

There are so many options out there when it comes to health and fitness, that it can be confusing and overwhelming to know who to trust for the right advice. MyTeam takes the pain out of this process by providing you a simple mentored journey to success by our team of coaches.

Expert coaches

MyTeam’s coaches are highly qualified specialists in their field. In addition, we have a network of dieticians & sport scientists who work with us to ensure all our programs are of the highest standards.

Teams - small group communities

A team consists of a small group of focused people pursuing the same goal, under the direction of their coach. Each team has a private forum where teammates share struggles and victories and ask questions in a supportive environment.

One-on-one

You’ll have a phone consultation with your coach as well as check ins and constant contact via the community. This provides accountability and ensures the program is always tailored correctly to your needs and goals.

Exercise program

Each team has a tailored exercise program engineered to achieve it's overall goal. When you join a team, your coach customises this program to ensure it fits your lifestyle & experience.

Nutritional guide

Diet plans are useless. Life was never meant to be strict and rigid. This is why MyTeam teaches you how to transition into healthy eating habits gradually, so it becomes a simple and enjoyable habit.

100% Guarantee

We believe in what we do, and we know we can get you the results you want. This is why we offer a full refund if you aren’t completely satisfied after 45 days.
